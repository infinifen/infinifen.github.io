fen's favorite times of day

in no particular order

(for your information)

assume it's april/may in the northern hemisphere as those are my favorite times of year

  • 9 pm (21:00)

    it's getting dark now. people start going out onto the streets and partying, but i'm not interested in that. you can take beautiful photos of the gradient-like sky with just your phone camera and a little pro mode fuckery. i personally love going out to more residential areas and just wandering around surrounded by the light from old sodium-powered lamps and no goddamn people!!!!

  • 2 am (2:00)

    it's basically the middle of the night. not mathematically, but from a human perspective. there's a good chance everybody else at home is asleep and you get to have some peace and be yourself and talk to people and stuff. other than that, there's not a lot of interesting stuff to be done as i don't really want to go out alone at 2am.

  • 4 am (4:00)

    okay, now everybody is asleep. except if you're one of those people who have to travel a moderately long distance to your job that starts at 6am but for my convenience i shall remain blissfully unaware of your existence (sorry). it's getting bright again and you could get even more sky gradienty shots if you went out.
